Monument in Bonn, Germany

This Beethoven Monument stands on the Münsterplatz in Bonn, Beethoven's birthplace. It was unveiled on August 12, 1845, in honor of the 75th anniversary of the composer's birth.

Opus 3 Handwritten Manuscript

"Beethoven’s personal life was marked by a struggle against deafness, and some of his most important works were composed during the last 10 years of his life, when he was quite unable to hear."

Beethoven House

The Beethoven House in Bonn, Germany, the centerpiece of the Beethoven-Haus is Beethoven's birthplace at Bonngasse 20. Founded in 1889 by the Beethoven-Haus association, it studies the life and work of the composer.
